Privacy Policy

Focus Friction Chrome Extension — Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Focus Friction does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data used by the extension stays on your device and within your own Google account.

Data stored

The only data stored by this extension is the list of websites you choose to add to your friction list. This list is saved using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API, which syncs the list across your own Chrome profiles via your Google account. It is never sent to any third-party server.

Data not collected

Focus Friction does not access, store, or transmit:

  • Browsing history
  • Page content or URLs you visit
  • Personally identifiable information
  • Any analytics or usage data

Third-party services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
